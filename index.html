WCC2024


31 OCTOBER – 3 NOVEMBER

The IBA World Cocktail Championship 2024 is upon us, get ready for the ultimate gathering of the world’s most exceptional Cocktail and Flair Bartenders! With representatives from all 67 countries of the IBA, the WCC promises an unparalleled showdown of talent and excitement. Prepare for an extravaganza of cocktails, skill, and cocktail entertainment!


Our hosting nation this year is Portugal on the tropical island of Madeira, led by the incredible ASSOCIAÇÃO BARMEN DE PORTUGAL.


National level Cocktail and Flair Competitions are being held during the year, with each of our 67 IBA Countries awarding their National Champions to represent their proud association on their journey to become the next IBA World Cocktail Champion.


As we prepare for our best WCC yet, we invite you to follow along the journeys of our cocktail Champions.


See you in Madeira 2024!
